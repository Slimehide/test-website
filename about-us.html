About Us

Who We Are

Calypsis is a Small Business Administration (SBA) certified Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB), proudly serving the United States Government, including the Intelligence Community and the Department of Defense.

We specialize in digital transformation solutions and consulting services that address the unique challenges of mission-critical environments. We are trusted to deliver secure, scalable, and future-ready systems.

Our team of highly skilled professionals combines modern technology with strategic project management to provide innovative yet pragmatic solutions. At Calypsis, we live by one purpose: Ignite Your Mission.

Why We’re Here

Our purpose is simple: to deliver solutions that ignite your mission and enable success where it matters most. Through innovation, integrity, and excellence, we aim to be the trusted partner for government agencies seeking transformation that drives mission success.
